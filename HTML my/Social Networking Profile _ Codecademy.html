Fancy up your font

Perfect. Your profile's really coming along!

It still looks a bit bland, though. Thankfully, you know how to fancify your fonts with the font-family, font-size, and color properties! Which is exactly what you're about to do.

Instructions

Spice up your profile with different fonts, sizes, and colors. You can do any combination you like, so long as you use font-family, font-size, and color at least once each!
